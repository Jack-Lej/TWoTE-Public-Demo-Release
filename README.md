INFO:

The Whites of Their Eyes is a resource-management level-based boomer shooter in the style of DOOM and Quake, with a twist: you never pick up any ammo or health items.

Instead, in order to progress to lower floors, you must discard your weapons and ammo to get under a weight limit.

The enemies get easier as the fight goes on, but will you make the right choices of what to discard in order to survive?

Make the best of what you've got; you'll never know how long you'll have to hold on to it.


CONTROLS:

Movement: W/A/S/D: Forward/Strafe Left/Backwards/Strafe Right

Look: Mouse to look

Fire Weapon: Left Click

Use Health Kit: H

Reload Weapon: R

Cycle Weapon Backward: Q

Cycle Weapon Forward: E

Switch Weapon: 1: Shotgun. 2: Rifle. 3: Railgun, 4 Rocket Launcer, 5: Minigun, 6: Sphere of Doom Launcher

Activate/Deactivate Weapon Drop UI: U

Activate/Deactivate Mouse Cursor: `
